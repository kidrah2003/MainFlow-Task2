About Safari Zoo

Safari Zoo is a world-renowned sanctuary committed to the preservation and protection of wildlife. Explore the rich diversity of animals and experience a close encounter with nature like never before.

Plan Your Visit

Safari Zoo is open daily from 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM. Tickets are available online and at the entrance. Join us for a journey into the wild!
